Local Airports

The 2 large airports most convenient to Princeton are Philadelphia International (PHL) and Newark Liberty International (EWR). There is also a small airport much closer to Princeton (Trenton-Mercer, TTN) that is used by Frontier airlines. The flight options tend to be limited, but if you find one that works for you it may be a good option.
