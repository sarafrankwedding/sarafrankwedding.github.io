It always brings us joy when, on an occasion for gift-giving, someone chooses to give in our honor to those who are in need. After all, we are blessed to already have what we need, and the principle of tzedakah (charity, or justice) is very important to us both.

Feel free to donate to the charity of your choice, and please tell us which charity you chose so we can know how to thank you. If you are looking for suggestions, here are some of our favorite charities:

GiveWell
World Central Kitchen
Doctors Without Borders
Engineers Without Borders
Habitat for Humanity

If you prefer to give a traditional gift, here is a link to a Google doc showing items we can use, which we may continue to edit as we think of more items: link. Please write your name next to the gift item you choose, and then please send the amount you wish to give by Zelle to sarafridov@gmail.com, or by check made out to Sara Fridovich-Keil or Chih-Yuan Chiu and mailed to 1292 Vista Leaf Drive, Decatur GA 30033. We will put your contribution toward the purchase of the item you selected and will send you a thank you photo of us holding the gift when it arrives. Thank you!
